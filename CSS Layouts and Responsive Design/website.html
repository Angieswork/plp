What is the Menstrual Cycle?

The menstrual cycle is a natural process where the body prepares for pregnancy. If no pregnancy occurs, the uterus sheds its lining, causing bleeding.

Common Symptoms

Many experience cramps, bloating, fatigue, headaches, and mood swings. Regular exercise and hydration can help ease discomfort.

Hygiene Tips

Use clean sanitary products, change them regularly, and maintain good personal hygiene to avoid infections.

Myths vs. Facts

❌ Myth: You can't exercise during your period.

✅ Fact: Light exercise can actually help reduce cramps and boost mood.

❌ Myth: Period blood is dirty.

✅ Fact: It's just normal blood mixed with the uterine lining.

❌ Myth: You can't swim on your period.

✅ Fact: With tampons or menstrual cups, you can swim without issues.

Menstrual Product Comparison

Pads

✅ Easy to use, great for beginners.

❌ Can feel bulky and uncomfortable.

Tampons

✅ Great for sports/swimming, discreet.

❌ Risk of Toxic Shock Syndrome (TSS) if not changed regularly.

Menstrual Cups

✅ Reusable, eco-friendly, cost-effective.

❌ Requires proper insertion and cleaning.
